Welcome to the Fruit by the Plate Project

Every morning Jafar Zaidi, 73 years old, picks fruit from his backyard for his morning breakfast. Part of this ritual is to wash, cut, and assemble a fruit plate into unique arrangement each day which he takes a photo of and shares with his family.

This website is a simple application that allows Jafar Zaidi to easily publish his daily fruit plate arrangements to Instagram.

The website allows him to log in to his `Fruit by the Plate` instagram account. Once logged in, the application stores his credentials and listens to text messages from his phone to a specific Twilio phone number. When it recieves a text message from that number with an image, it automatically uploads the image and caption to his `Fruit by the Plate` instagram account.
